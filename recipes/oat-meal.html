Oat meal recipe

The meal for bodybuilders!

Oat meal has undoubtedly many benefits for our body, especially for bodybuilders.

I personally benefit the most from the amount of carbohydrates available in oats to power my body and energize me before every workout so that I can perform my best at the gym!

Ingredients

  • Oats, obviously. ;D
  • Some dried grabes, or your favorite dried fruit.
  • A few almonds.
  • A banana.
  • 300ml of milk
  • A scoop of your favorite protein powder.

Steps for making the meal

  1. Simply mix all the ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Put the meal in a microwave for a while if you love eating it warm. I personally use cold milk and eat it while it is cold!
  3. Thats it! Enjoy your meal!
