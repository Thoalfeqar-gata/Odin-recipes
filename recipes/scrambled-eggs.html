Scrambled eggs

Scrambled eggs. A ton of protein basically!

For those of you who want a huge amount of protein to give your muscles the building blocks that they need to grow bigger and stronger after a hard workout, you can't go wrong with scrambled eggs!

A single egg has anywhere between 4-7 grams of protein depending on its size.

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs (I usually cook 5 eggs).
  • 1 tomato.
  • 150g of diced onions.
  • 100g of green sweet pepper.
  • A teaspoon of olive oil.
  • Salt and cumen.

Cooking steps

  1. Cook the onions until they turn brownish.
  2. Add the tomato and pepper after dicing them into small pieces.
  3. Close the lid of the pan and reduce the heat to the lowest. Cook them for about 20min, and add a small amount of water if you think that it is not steamy enough.
  4. After the moisture dries, add the eggs (make sure to mix them and add the salt and pepper first!)
  5. Make sure to evenly cook everything by mixing while cooking until the egg reaches the level of softness you like!
  6. Enjoy your meal sir!
